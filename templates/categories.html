UN YOUTH VIDEO CONTEST

Eligibility:UPTO 25 YEARS
LAST DATE:JUNE 19,2020

Organized by the United Nations Alliance of Civilizations (UNAOC), this competition invites students to submit a video on the topic of migration, diversity, social inclusion and the prevention of xenophobia. Submissions can be made in three categories - First (up to 12 years old), Second (13-17 years old) and Third (17-25 years old). Up to 6 winners will be selected for a fully funded trip to Morocco for the annual award ceremony.

TAKHTE 1AC ONLINE ESSAY CONTEST

Eligibility:11 - 24 YEARS
Last Date:JUNE 30, 2020

UNESCO and Takhte jointly invite young people to engage in a reflection - through words - on some of the most important questions facing humanity with a special focus on the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. Essays should not be in more than 700 words and can be submitted in either of the two categories - Children (11-14 years) and Youth (15-24 Years). 100 winning essays will be published in the form of a book entitled Year 1 AC (After Coronavirus): ESSAYS BY 100 YOUNG INDIANS.

Eligibility:18 YEARS OR ABOVE
Last Date:JUNE 30, 2020

The competition aims to encourage students and entrepreneurs to come forward with new ideas to help improve the construction industry. Applicants in the Undergraduate category need to submit an essay or a video and applicants in the open category are required to answer a set of questions regarding their ideas.
